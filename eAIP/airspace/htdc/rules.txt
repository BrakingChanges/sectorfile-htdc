===== HTDC Dar-es-Salaam Airspace Rules =====

==== Separation ====

- RNAV 10 supports 50 NM lateral and the 50 NM longitudinal distance based separation minima in oceanic or remote area airspace. 


==== SELCAL ====

  * SELCAL is NOT available within the HTDC FIR airspace.

==== SLOP ====

  * SLOP is possible within the HTDC FIR airspace.


==== VFR Rules ====

  * VFR flights shall not be operadored above FL145 without approval.
