==== FRA Waypoints ====

  * Controllers may redirect any flights from/to a FRA Entry/Exit Waypoint to a FRA Arrival/Departure Connecting Waypoint or from a FRA Entry Waypoint to a FRA Exit Waypoint.
  * Controllers may never redirect any flight that may cross Restricted or Special User Airspace. In those circumstances, controllers may use the FRA Intermediate Waypoints to redirect the flights.
