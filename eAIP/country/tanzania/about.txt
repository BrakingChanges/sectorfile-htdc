===== Tanzania Information =====

Tanzania, officially the United Republic of Tanzania, is a country in East Africa within the African Great Lakes region. It borders [[country:uganda:home|Uganda]] to the north; [[country:kenya:home|Kenya]] to the northeast; the Indian Ocean to the east; [[country:mozambique:home|Mozambique]] and [[country:malawi:home|Malawi]] to the south; [[country:zambia:home|Zambia]] to the southwest; and [[country:rwanda:home|Rwanda]], [[country:burundi:home|Burundi]], and the [[country:congo-dr:home|Democratic Republic of the Congo]] to the west. Mount Kilimanjaro, Africa's highest mountain, is in northeastern Tanzania. According to the United Nations, Tanzania has a population of 63.59 million, making it the most populous country located entirely south of the equator. 

For more information, please visit [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tanzania|Wikipedia]].
