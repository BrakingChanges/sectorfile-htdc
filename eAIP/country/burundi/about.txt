==== Burundi Information ====

Burundi, officially the Republic of Burundi, is a landlocked country in the Great Rift Valley at the junction between the African Great Lakes region and East Africa. It is bordered by [[country:rwanda:home|Rwanda]] to the north, [[country:tanzania:home|Tanzania]] to the east and southeast, and the [[country:congo-dr:home|Democratic Republic of the Congo]] to the west; Lake Tanganyika lies along its southwestern border. The capital cities are Gitega and Bujumbura, the latter being the country's largest city. 

For more information, please visit [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Burundi|Wikipedia]].
